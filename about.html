ABOUT US



A REVOLUTIONARY START

Revolution was established in 2020 as an effort to bring spin to the masses by making classes accessible, fun and enjoyable by people from all walks of life.

Within a year of launch, we have since expanded to two other studios locations, Orchard and Tanjong Pagar, becoming Singapore’s fastest growing spin phenomenon.

As our community grows, Revolution remains committed in riding with you as you become the best version of yourself.
